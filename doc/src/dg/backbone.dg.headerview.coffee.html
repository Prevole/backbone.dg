¶ Dg.HeaderView

As the Dg.RowView, the HeaderView is incomplete and expects to be extended for your own data.

This view offers the mechanism to sort the column of your data collection when they are presented in <table /> tag. Table headers HTML tags are used to render the headers.

The multi-sort is possible by pressing on shift key when clicks are done on the different columns.
